Privacy Policy

See Before Buy AI

Last Updated: 27 February 2026

1. Introduction

See Before Buy AI (“App”, “we”, “our”, or “us”) is a Shopify application that provides AI-powered virtual try-on functionality for online stores. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, process, and protect information when merchants install and use the app, and when customers interact with the AI try-on feature.

By installing or using See Before Buy AI, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

2. Information We Collect
A. Information We Collect from Merchants (Store Owners)

When a merchant installs See Before Buy AI, we collect and process the following information through Shopify’s API:

Store name

Store URL

Shopify store ID

Store email address

Shopify access tokens

Product information (titles, images, variants, pricing)

Order information, including historical orders (if permission is granted)

Order data may include:

Order ID

Product details

Order value

Currency

Purchase timestamps

We only access the minimum required data necessary to operate app functionality and analytics.

B. Information Processed from Store Customers

When a customer uses the “See Before You Buy” feature on a product page, we may process:

Uploaded photos/images

Selected product details

AI-generated preview images

Add-to-cart interactions

Purchase confirmation data (for analytics purposes only)

Customer images are processed solely for generating virtual try-on previews.

We do not collect biometric identifiers, do not perform facial recognition, and do not build user identity profiles.

3. How We Use Information

We use collected data strictly for the following purposes:

Generating AI try-on preview images

Displaying virtual previews on product pages

Enabling add-to-cart functionality after try-on

Tracking try-on events

Tracking add-to-cart events

Measuring conversion rates

Calculating revenue attribution

Displaying analytics in the merchant dashboard

Improving AI generation performance

Providing merchant support

If order access older than 60 days is granted, historical order data is used only to:

Provide accurate revenue analytics

Calculate long-term performance metrics

Display historical conversion insights

We do not use merchant or customer data for advertising purposes.

4. Image Processing & Storage

Customer-uploaded images:

Are securely transmitted using encrypted connections

Are processed to generate AI previews

Are not used for facial recognition

Are not sold, shared, or publicly distributed

Images may be temporarily stored for processing and quality purposes and are automatically deleted after a limited retention period unless required for operational or security reasons.

5. Data Security

We implement commercially reasonable security measures, including:

HTTPS encrypted API communication

Secure Shopify OAuth authentication

Restricted internal data access

Secure cloud hosting infrastructure

While we take appropriate steps to protect information, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade merchant or customer data.

We may share limited data only:

With secure infrastructure or AI processing providers strictly to operate app functionality

When required by law, legal process, or government request

To comply with Shopify platform requirements

All service providers are required to maintain appropriate data protection standards.

7. Data Retention

We retain merchant and order data only as long as necessary to:

Provide app functionality

Maintain analytics accuracy

Fulfill legal obligations

If a merchant uninstalls See Before Buy AI, associated store data will be deleted within a reasonable period, unless retention is required for legal or compliance purposes.

Customer-uploaded images are automatically deleted after processing or within a limited storage window.

Access to stored store data

Correction of inaccurate data

Deletion of their store data

9. International Data Transfers

Data may be processed on secure cloud servers located outside the merchant’s country. By using the app, merchants consent to such data transfers where necessary to provide the service.

10. Children’s Privacy

See Before Buy AI is not directed toward individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.
